# FU Berlin M.Sc. Data Science – Degree Structure

Source: FU-Mitteilungen 18/2021, Studien- und Prüfungsordnung für den gemeinsamen Masterstudiengang Data Science.

## Basic degree info

- Degree: Master of Science (M.Sc.)
- University: Freie Universität Berlin
- Faculties:
  - Fachbereich Mathematik und Informatik
  - Fachbereich Erziehungswissenschaft und Psychologie
- Standard duration: 4 semesters
- Total workload: 120 LP

## Overall structure

The program consists of:

| Component | LP |
|---|---:|
| Grundlagenbereich / foundation area | 30 LP |
| Profilbereich / profile area | 60 LP |
| Masterarbeit with accompanying colloquium | 30 LP |
| Total | 120 LP |

## Foundation area: 30 LP

All students must complete the following mandatory modules:

| Module | LP |
|---|---:|
| Introduction to Profile Areas | 5 LP |
| Statistics for Data Science | 10 LP |
| Machine Learning for Data Science | 10 LP |
| Programming for Data Science | 5 LP |

## Profile area: choose exactly one profile

Students must choose and complete one of two profiles:

1. Data Science in Life Sciences
2. Data Science Technologies

The chosen profile is determined by taking the corresponding mandatory modules.

Important constraint:
Modules chosen in the profile elective area must not be identical to modules already completed in the Bachelor’s degree. In doubtful cases, the Prüfungsausschuss decides.

# Master thesis

| Requirement | Rule |
|---|---|
| LP | 30 LP |
| Includes | Masterarbeit with accompanying colloquium |
| Admission requirement | at least 60 LP completed in the program |
| Topic area | Data Science |
| Length | approx. 70 pages |
| Working time | 23 weeks |
| Language | English by default; German possible only with approved request |
| Colloquium | normally one approx. 30-minute talk on thesis progress |
| External thesis | possible with approval, if academic supervision is ensured |

## Graduation requirement

The degree is completed when the required achievements under § 7 and § 9 are fulfilled:

- 30 LP Grundlagenbereich
- 60 LP Profilbereich
- 30 LP Masterarbeit with colloquium
- no exclusion case under § 14(2)

After successful completion, the degree awarded is:

Master of Science (M.Sc.)
