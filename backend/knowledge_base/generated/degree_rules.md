# FU Berlin Master Informatik Degree Rules

Source: `ressources/Informatik_Master_Ablauf.txt` and the 2014 checklist PDF.

## Overall structure

The regular study duration is 4 semesters.

The Master Informatik comprises 120 LP total:

- 90 LP modules before the thesis
- 30 LP Masterarbeit

There are no compulsory modules.

## Informatik area

The Informatik area must contain 70 to 80 LP. It consists of three study areas:

- Praktische Informatik
- Technische Informatik
- Theoretische Informatik

Exactly one of these three areas must be selected as the specialization area.

Minimum LP requirements:

- Praktische Informatik: at least 20 LP, or at least 40 LP if it is the specialization area
- Technische Informatik: at least 10 LP, or at least 30 LP if it is the specialization area
- Theoretische Informatik: at least 10 LP, or at least 30 LP if it is the specialization area

## Anwendungsbereich

The Anwendungsbereich, formerly Nebenfach, must contain at least 10 LP and at most 20 LP.

## Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten

Students must complete at least 2 and at most 4 modules from the Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten modules in the core Informatik selection.

At least one Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten module must be from the specialization area.

Additional Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten modules can be placed in the Wahlbereich. Because the Wahlbereich has 10 LP and seminars have 5 LP each, this can allow up to 2 additional Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten modules beyond the normal maximum.

The same exact Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten module, for example "Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten Technische Informatik A", can only be counted once.

## Softwareprojekt

Students must complete at least 1 and at most 2 Softwareprojekt modules in the core Informatik selection.

An additional Softwareprojekt can be placed in the Wahlbereich. Because the Wahlbereich has 10 LP and a Softwareprojekt has 10 LP, this can allow 3 Softwareprojekt modules total when one of them is counted in the Wahlbereich.

In the Master Informatik, Softwareprojekt A is graded and Softwareprojekt B is ungraded.

## Ungraded modules

The total amount of modules with non-differentiated assessment or no assessment must be 25 to 30 LP.

Ungraded modules include:

- all Wissenschaftliches Arbeiten modules, 5 LP each
- Softwareprojekt B, 10 LP
- Grundlagen des Managements von IT-Projekten, 5 LP
- Projektmanagement, 5 LP
- Projektmanagement - Vertiefung, 5 LP
- Praktiken professioneller Softwareentwicklung, 5 LP
- Existenzgründung in der IT-Industrie, 5 LP

Modules from the Anwendungsbereich also count toward this limit when they are ungraded.

## Bachelor modules

Modules from a Bachelor program can only be included up to 15 LP.

Bachelor modules listed in the checklist include:

- Grundlagen des Managements von IT-Projekten, 5 LP
- Existenzgründung in der IT-Industrie, 5 LP

Application-area modules may also be Bachelor modules and must be included in the 15 LP limit when applicable.

## Duplicate modules

The same exact module cannot count twice in the Master.

The same Master module that was already used in a prior Bachelor specialization cannot be reused for the Master.
